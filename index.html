Structural direction

Each month we track dozens of membership metrics. The MMM consolidates them into one consistent view so we see direction, not just activity. Elections, resignations, and billing timing don't always line up — a single month can look unusually strong or weak. The MMM shows what's normal and what's truly changing. We look at all three, but decisions are based on pace, not one month.

Why Multiple Horizons

Any single month can look stronger or weaker than it really is. A large election class, a billing timing shift, or an unusual resignation cluster can all distort the picture.

The rolling averages account for those one-time variations so the report shows what's genuinely changing in membership — not just what happened to fall in this particular month.
